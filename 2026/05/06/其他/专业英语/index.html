1.2 语句

The factors that make concrete a universal building material are so pronounced that it has been used, in more primitive kinds and ways than at present, for thousands of years, starting with lime mortars from 12,000 to 6000 BCE in Crete, Cyprus, Greece, and the Middle East. (Unit 1)

使混凝土成为一种通用建筑材料的因素非常显著，以至于它在更原始的形式和方式下已被使用了数千年，早在公元前 12,000 年至公元前 6,000 年的克里特岛、塞浦路斯、希腊和中东地区就开始使用石灰砂浆。

The reinforcement, usually round steel rods with appropriate surface deformations to provide interlocking, is placed in the forms in advance of the concrete. (Unit 1)

钢筋，通常是具有适当表面变形以提供咬合的圆钢棒，在混凝土浇注前预先放置在模具中。

Concrete is a stonelike material obtained by permitting a carefully proportioned mixture of cement, sand and gravel or other aggregate, and water to harden in forms of the shape and dimensions of the desired structure. (Unit 1)

混凝土是一种类似石材的材料，通过将水泥、砂、砾石或其他骨料与水按精心配比混合，并在所需结构的形状和尺寸的模板中硬化而获得。

Most of the constituent materials, with the exception of cement and additives, are usually available at low cost locally or at small distances from the construction site. (Unit 1)

除了水泥和添加剂外，大多数组成材料通常可以在当地或离施工现场不远的地方以低成本获得。

To serve its purpose, a structure must be safe against collapse and serviceable in use. (Unit 1)

为了达到其目的，结构必须在使用中安全而不倒塌，并且具有适用性。

A prestressed concrete member can be defined as one in which there have been introduced internal stresses of such magnitude and distribution that the stresses resulting from the given external loading are counteracted to a desired degree. (Unit 2)

预应力混凝土构件可以被定义为引入了大小的内部应力、并且其分布使得给定外部荷载产生的应力被抵消到期望程度的构件。

Prestressing applies a precompression to the member that reduces or eliminates undesirable tensile stresses that would otherwise be present. (Unit 2)

预应力对构件施加预压力，以减少或消除原本存在的不利的拉应力。

Such systems can generally be classified as pretensioning or post-tensioning systems. (Unit 2)

这些系统通常可分为先张法或后张法系统。

2.2 语句

Structural design may be defined as a mixture of art and science, combining the experienced engineer’s intuitive feeling for the behavior of a structure with a sound knowledge of the principles of statics, dynamics, mechanics of materials, and structural analysis, to produce a safe economical structure that will serve its intended purpose. (Unit 3)

结构设计可以被定义为艺术与科学的结合，将经验丰富的工程师对结构行为的直觉与对静力学、动力学、材料力学和结构分析原理的扎实知识相结合，以生产出安全、经济并能达到预期目的的结构。

Design is a process by which an optimum solution is obtained. (Unit 3)

设计是一个获得最优解的过程。

Dead load is a fixed-position gravity service load, so called because it acts continuously toward the earth when the structure is in service. (Unit 3)

恒载是一种固定位置的重力使用荷载，之所以如此称呼，是因为它在结构服役期间持续朝向地心作用。

Gravity loads acting when the structure is in service, but varying in magnitude and location, are termed live loads. (Unit 3)

结构在使用期间作用的大小和位置变化的重力荷载称为活荷载。

The term impact as ordinarily used in structural design refers to the dynamic effect of a suddenly applied load. (Unit 3)

结构设计中通常使用的”冲击”一词指的是突然施加荷载的动力效应。

The function of a structure is the principal factor determining the structural configuration. (Unit 3)

结构的功能是决定结构构型的主要因素。

3.2 语句

Earthquakes result from the sudden movement of tectonic plates in the earth’s crust. (Unit 4)

地震是由地壳中构造板块的突然运动引起的。

The purpose of seismic design is to proportion structures so that they can withstand the displacements and the forces induced by the ground motion. (Unit 4)

抗震设计的目的是按比例设计结构，使其能够承受地面运动引起的位移和力。

The safety of a structure subjected to seismic loading rests on the designer’s understanding of the response of the structure to ground motion. (Unit 4)

承受地震荷载的结构安全性取决于设计者对结构对地面运动响应的理解。

The closer the frequency of the ground motion is to one of the natural frequencies of a structure, the greater the likelihood of the structure experiencing resonance. (Unit 4)

地面运动的频率越接近结构的某一自然频率，结构发生共振的可能性就越大。

Members designed for seismic loading must perform in a ductile fashion and dissipate energy in a manner that does not compromise the strength of the structure. (Unit 4)

为地震荷载设计的构件必须以延性方式工作，并以不损害结构强度的方式耗散能量。

For a frame undergoing lateral displacement, the flexural capacity of the members at a joint should be such that the columns are stronger than the beams. (Unit 4)

对于承受侧向位移的框架，节点处构件的抗弯承载力应使柱比梁更强。

4.2 语句

Composite construction is a method of construction that is used in a variety of engineering and building applications. (Unit 5)

组合结构是一种用于各种工程和建筑应用中的施工方法。

In structural engineering, composite construction exists when two different materials are bound together so strongly that they act together as a single unit from a structural point of view. (Unit 5)

在结构工程中，当两种不同材料被牢固地结合在一起，以至于从结构角度看它们作为一个整体共同工作时，就存在组合结构。

Composite steel-concrete beam is made up of steel beam and slab by shear connector. (Unit 5)

钢-混凝土组合梁由钢梁和楼板通过抗剪连接件组成。

Concrete-filled tube columns consist of a steel tube filled with concrete. (Unit 5)

钢管混凝土柱由填充混凝土的钢管组成。

Due to the benefit of composite action of the two materials, the CFT columns provide excellent seismic event resistant structural properties such as high strength, high ductility and large energy absorption capacity. (Unit 5)

由于两种材料的组合作用优势，钢管混凝土柱提供了优异的抗震结构性能，如高强度、高延性和大能量吸收能力。

Therefore, composite slabs which combine the advantages of cast-in-place slabs and precast slabs become popular. (Unit 5)

因此，结合现浇板和预制板优点的组合板变得流行起来。

7.2 语句

The composite structural system for the Jin Mao Tower located in Shanghai, China was designed to resist typhoon winds, earthquake forces, and accommodate poor soil conditions while providing a very slender tower to be fully occupied for the office and hotel uses. (Unit 18)

位于中国上海的金茂大厦组合结构体系旨在抵抗台风、地震作用，并适应不良的地基条件，同时提供一座非常细长的、可供办公和酒店全面使用的高楼。

The superstructure of the Tower resists lateral loads with a central reinforced concrete shear wall core interconnected with composite mega-columns. (Unit 18)

该塔楼的上部结构通过中央钢筋混凝土剪力墙核心筒与组合巨柱相互连接来抵抗侧向荷载。

Because of the extremely poor upper-strata soil conditions, deep, high-capacity structural steel pipe piles are required to transfer the superstructure loads to the soil by friction. (Unit 18)

由于极差的浅层地基条件，需要采用深层的、高承载力的结构钢管桩，通过摩擦力将上部结构荷载传递到地基。

Wind tunnel studies, performed under the direction of Dr. Nichol Isyumov at University of Western Ontario in conjunction with the Shanghai Climate Center, were conducted for the building located in the existing site condition. (Unit 18)

在安大略大学 Nichol Isyumov 博士的指导下，并与上海气候中心合作，对该建筑在现有场地条件下进行了风洞研究。

The approach for evaluating seismic loadings for the Jin Mao Tower considers both Chinese Code-defined seismic criteria and actual site-specific geological, tectonic, seismological and soil characteristics. (Unit 18)

金茂大厦地震荷载评估方法同时考虑了中国规范定义的抗震标准和实际场地特定的地质、构造、地震学和土壤特征。

Incorporating fundamental structural engineering concepts into the final design of the Jin Mao Tower lead to a solution which not only addressed the adverse site conditions but also provided an efficient final design. (Unit 18)

将基本结构工程概念融入金茂大厦的最终设计，得出的解决方案不仅解决了不利的场地条件，而且提供了高效的最终设计。
